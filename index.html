About
Us

As the Nation's largest Black Owned media company, Urban One
has been Black America's advocate for the last 40 years. Through
various media outlets, including TV One, Radio One, Global Grind,
MadameNoire and many more, they create constant awareness of
national disparities. One disparity that remains unchanged is the
dysfunctional relationship between financial institutions and the
black community. It's no secret systemic discrimination has led
Black Americans to shy away from banks, creating a sentiment of
distrust and unpredictability of a traditional banking relationship. As
Urban One continues to advocate for the betterment of Black
Americans, they created ONE VIP, a trusted digital banking service
that is predictable, accessible and recirculates black wealth.


Black buying power in America is over $1.2 trillion dollars. Just like
Black Lives Matter, Black Money Matters too. ONE VIP's Rewards
program is focused on rewarding members for buying black;
keeping black dollars in the black community.


Our aim is to create financial freedom for the Black community by
connecting people with money, values and experiences. We believe
in de-bunking negative narratives around money, simplifying daily
money matters so we have more time for life's rewarding stuff.
